About Us



Our mentor's vision for starting CLAT APEX.


Law is not merely a profession but it is a tool for the well being of the society.
Law entrance exams have been giving a way to the law aspirants to fulfill their dream of studying at a top law school.
We believe every student has its unique way of preparation and should be mentored according to their needs. A competitive exam requires hard work, dedication along with confidence.

The personal mentoring programme of CLAT APEX makes sure that each and every student is well prepared as well as confident enough to crack the exam.The limited no.of students will create a difference from the rest of the coaching which would allow the mentor for personal attention to every student. The personal guidance of the team will ensure a healthy environment for the process of learning.

We at Clat Apex aim to make clat accessible to all who are disadvantaged in terms of location and economic condition.

CLAT APEX is an institute which will make you special for ever so that you use your skills in your domain forever. For more information meet our founder, mentor JYOTSHNA YASHASWI at CLAT APEX.
